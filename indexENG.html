Our Tech

Parameterized Smart Contracts

We will implement fault-tolerant Byzantine consensus algorithms to ensure authenticated and secure transactions through smart contracts.

Disintermediation by Decentralization.

Our decentralized P2P architecture reduces the need for intermediaries, empowering a more efficient and autonomous marketplace of individuals.

Immutable Validation Record.

We will use Merkle Trees techniques to ensure full cryptographic traceability of every transaction, enabling transparent and verifiable audits.

Single account per human

We count on the integration and technological support of WordCoin to guarantee a unique record per person, preserving anonymity.

Cryptographic Transaction Security

The blockchain implements cryptographic hash algorithms and proof-of-work to ensure that every transaction on the freelancer marketplace maintains its integrity over time.

Mutual Credits

1. Pricing: The market decides.Prices are quoted in multiple cryptocurrencies and dollars, allowing flexibility and accessibility.

2. Ecosystem Balance: We have income and expenses, like any system. But here, everything is transparent.Commissions are calculated based on price and loyalty levels, and if you use our native token (EMP), commissions are halved!

3. Mutual Credits and Tokens: No funds? No problem. We issue credit based on your behavior and needs. EMP and DEUS tokens facilitate exchanges, linking the real value of services.

4 Token Burning Policy: To maintain equilibrium, we repurchase and burn tokens. This ensures that supply and demand are always in sync.

5. Adaptability: The system adjusts to market conditions and community needs through data-driven consensus. Allowing the model to evolve and adapt, rather than become obsolete.
